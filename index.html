1. HEALTH EXPRESS INTRODUCTION:

Allcare Express Health is a new initiative introduced by Allcare that aims to revolutionize the way customers interact with their pharmacy. The initiative is focused on enhancing convenience, speed, and satisfaction for customers, and it is achieved through the use of our new cutting-edge Mobile App that enables customers to place Express Orders and pay remotely through a secure SMS payment link.

Allcare's goal is to create an exceptional customer experience that sets them apart from the competition and strengthens customer loyalty. To achieve this goal, Allcare has implemented a new workflow that allows customers to conveniently order items, pay, collect, and go, and they have provided guidelines for stores to follow to ensure the success of the Allcare Express Health Initiative.

Allcare Express Health is not merely an app or payment method, but rather a comprehensive experience that encompasses a streamlined in-store medication acquisition process, designed to enhance the brand's overall image, reputation, and customer experience. By providing an integrated journey that exceeds the individual components, Allcare aspires to create a seamless experience for its customers. Currently in a pilot phase at six locations, we aim to learn, adapt, and ultimately expand this successful program to all of our stores.

Initial Goals of Trial: Measure the uptake of Express Health Experience by Patients. Measure Patient Feedback, Store Experience and Iterate Enhancements for full store rollout.

Note: Post trial, parts of this process will be automated with phase 2 upgrades and integrations based on feedback from participating stores.

3. FAQ & TRAINING SUPPORT

Click on the "Send Payment Request" button to see a Pop Up appear where you can enter the Patients phone number and/or email. Click "Create" on the pop up when all fields are completed to send immediately to patient.

To take a payment over the phone, click on the "Take Payment" button on left corner of your Med-Pay Dashboard. Simply fill in the customers details on the pop and credit card info to process a payment.

To know if a patient has paid, simply login to your Med-Pay store dashboard. Click on "Requests" button on the left. All will be listed here marked Paid or Unpaid with filtering and search options.

Issuing a refund is very simple from the dashboard. Once a refund is issued, a receipt of refund will be sent to the customer for their records. If you need to issue a refund to a customer follow the steps below. How to issue a refund from the dashboard: 1. Locate the desired payment under the "Payments" tab 2. Ensure the customer name, email and amount is correct 3. To the far right of the payment, select the "X" symbol under "Actions" 4. Select Confirm 5. A confirmation of refund will be sent to your customer automatically. The payment will then be shown by showing a date under the "Date Refunded" column.

To see if your customer has received or viewed your payment request - click on Requests Tab. The dashboard makes it easy to see if a payment request has been viewed, received or paid. It also allows you to send additional "Nudges" to your customer to prompt payment. For all payments received or payment requests paid, you will be notified by the App or by email also. To See if a Request has been Paid: Go to the "Request" tab on your dashboard Locate the request you are looking for Along the top bar, you will see a "Status" Under "Status" you will see if the request is "Paid" or "Unpaid" To the right of the "Status" column, you will see a "Views" column. The number under the "Views" refers to how many times the link has been opened If the request has been opened from the requesting account, these views will also count towards this total By selection the downward arrow under "Actions" you can also download the invoice for payment, send a nudge or resend a receipt manually.

With our automated receipt feature, you no longer need to worry about creating custom receipts and invoices for customers. Receipts are automatically generated and sent to your customers when a payment is captured. In the case of payment requests, an invoice is generated outlining the payment due. However if you need to re-send a receipt follow the below steps: To Re-Send A Receipt 1. Locate the payment in the "Payments" tab on your dashboard 2. To the right of the payment there will be an arrow symbol pointing to the right 3. Select the arrow and click "Confirm" 4. The receipt will be re-sent to the customer To View A Receipt 1. Locate the payment in the "Payments" tab on your dashboard 2. To the right of the payment there will be an arrow pointing down 3. Select the arrow 4. A new browser page will load showing the receipt

If you have sent a customer a payment request and they would prefer to pay in cash or by bank transfer, there is a simple way to mark the payment as "Paid" on your dashboard. This will allow you to reconcile payments in an easier way and will ensure the payment request is disabled to avoid double payment. To mark a payment request as paid: 1. Go to the "Requests" tab on your dashboard 2. Locate the Request you wish to mark as "Paid" 3. Click the "Check" icon beside the payment

Our recurring payments feature allows all businesses to set up weekly, monthly or annual recurring payments such as subscriptions, payment plans and debt repayments without the need to set up direct debits or manually charge the customer. Once a recurring payment is set up, the amount will be pulled from the customers card at the specific frequency for the length of time chosen. Recurring payments can be created from both our Take and Request sections by toggling the payment type to "Recurring" and can be completed on desktop or on the App. How to create a recurring payment 1. Simply toggle the "Recurring Payment" button when inputting payment details, 2. Select the period (weekly, monthly, annually) and the duration (How many weeks, months or years the payment should be charged for). 3. Send the Request or Take the payment as normal, the recurring payment will now be set up. 4. You can view all recurring payments under the "Recurring" tab on your dashboard. 5. If a recurring payment fails, you will be notified by email. NOTE RECURRING PAYMENTS WILL NOT BE ACTIVE FOR THE TRIAL

Payment requests already operate on an automatic reminder sequence. However, you can also send a reminder manually from the dashboard. To Send a Nudge: 1. Locate the request under the "Requests" tab on your dashboard 2. To the right of the payment you will see a Bell Icon (pictured) 3. Click the bell icon 4. Click confirm A reminder will be resent to the customer to their email

The only difference in the delivery process is that you need to request and have received payment before placing the order in the Van. This is to ensure payment has been received so that delivery can be fulfilled.

The recommend process for this is to only label a bag with the "Express Health" sticker once the payment has been received. The Online Team with marketing will provide stickers all stores. First apply the sticker to a bag where the customer has opted for Express Health service. There is a tick box for you to mark the item paid on the sticker when you receive payment. This way you can know even between shifts what bags are still awaiting payment vs those that are paid. These stickers are also to provide the Customer a rewarding feeling to those Customers for choosing and experiencing Express Health from Allcare.
